Frequently Asked Questions

Q

How long is the demo?

Demos typically run 30 minutes. If you'd like more time to go deep on specific features or ask detailed compliance questions, just let us know in the notes field.

Q

Is it really free and no obligation?

Yes. There's no cost and no pressure to sign up. We want you to make an informed decision — if CertSync isn't the right fit, we'll tell you.

Q

What do I need to prepare?

Nothing. Just show up. If you have specific pain points or want to see a particular workflow, mentioning it in the notes field helps us tailor the session — but it's completely optional.

Q

How is the demo delivered?

Online via video call (Google Meet or Zoom — whichever you prefer). We'll send you a link in the confirmation email once we've locked in a time.

Q

Do you work with large or enterprise organisations?

Yes. For organisations with 50+ employees or complex multi-site requirements, we also offer tailored enterprise demos and custom pricing. Learn more about custom plans.

Q

What industries does CertSync cover?

CertSync is built for any Australian business with credential, licence, or permit compliance obligations — most commonly construction, mining, labour hire, facility management, healthcare, and logistics. If your team needs to stay WorkSafe or AHPRA compliant, we're likely a good fit.

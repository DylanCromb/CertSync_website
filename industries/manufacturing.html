Manufacturing workforce records

Manufacturing compliance gets messy across workers, contractors and equipment

Manufacturing teams manage forklift licences, plant tickets, equipment training, permits, inductions, contractor evidence, insurance documents and audit records. When files sit across shared drives, spreadsheets and emails, it becomes hard to know what is current, missing or expired.

CertSync gives operations, safety and admin teams one register for the records they already manage, without replacing WHS advice, safety procedures or regulator guidance.

The practical question CertSync helps answer

Can we quickly see whether worker, contractor, licence, plant ticket, training, permit and insurance records are current, missing or expired when work, audits or insurer reviews require evidence?

Why not spreadsheets?

Spreadsheets work until production records start expiring

Manufacturing records move across workers, roles, contractors, equipment and renewal dates. A spreadsheet can list them, but it rarely keeps the file, owner, status and expiry logic together.

Spreadsheet chasing

  • Expiry visibility depends on manual upkeep
  • Files live away from status fields
  • Training and permit records get separated
  • Audit packs take time to rebuild
  • Missing renewals are easy to overlook

CertSync register

  • Expiry dates stay attached to the record
  • Files, worker and status live together
  • Workers, teams and document types can be filtered
  • Evidence can be exported by useful views

Manufacturing compliance software FAQs

What is manufacturing compliance software?

Manufacturing compliance software helps teams organise worker licences, forklift tickets, plant training, permits, inductions, contractor documents, evidence files and expiry dates in one register.

What manufacturing records can CertSync track?

CertSync can track worker licences, forklift and plant tickets, role training, permits, inductions, contractor documents, insurance evidence, audit records and expiry dates.

Can CertSync track forklift and plant training records?

Yes. CertSync can store forklift licences, plant tickets, VOC evidence, equipment training records and renewal dates where your team manages those records.

Can CertSync track contractor documents for manufacturing sites?

Yes. CertSync can help organise contractor company records, worker credentials, insurances, permits, inductions and evidence files.

Can CertSync send manufacturing record expiry reminders?

Yes. CertSync can help teams monitor expiry dates and send reminders for licences, tickets, permits, training records, insurance documents and other time-sensitive evidence.

Can CertSync help prepare records for audits?

CertSync helps teams organise credential evidence and export reports by worker, team, record type, status or expiry window.

Does CertSync provide manufacturing safety or legal advice?

No. CertSync is a credential, expiry and evidence-management platform. It does not provide WHS consulting, legal advice, safety advice or regulatory approval.

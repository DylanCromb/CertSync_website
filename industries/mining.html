Mining workforce records

Mining workforce compliance gets messy across sites, roles and contractors

Mining and mining-adjacent teams often manage site inductions, plant tickets, high-risk work licences, VOCs, training records, permits, contractor documents and evidence requests across changing crews. When those records sit in spreadsheets, inboxes and shared folders, managers lose visibility.

CertSync gives site, office and contractor managers one register for the records they already manage, without replacing safety advice, site requirements or regulator guidance.

The practical question CertSync helps answer

Can we quickly see whether worker, contractor, induction, licence, plant ticket, permit, training and site evidence records are current, missing or expired before mobilisation or site access?

Mining compliance software FAQs

What is mining workforce compliance software?

Mining workforce compliance software helps teams organise worker, contractor, induction, licence, plant ticket, permit, training and site evidence records so managers can see what is current, missing or expired.

What mining workforce documents can CertSync track?

CertSync can track site inductions, role-specific training, high-risk work licences, plant and operator tickets, VOCs, permits, first aid records, emergency response certificates, contractor documents and expiry dates.

Can CertSync track site inductions and contractor evidence?

Yes. CertSync can help teams organise site inductions, contractor company records, worker evidence, insurances, permits, training records and other files your team needs to manage.

Can managers see expired and soon-to-expire mining records?

Yes. CertSync is designed to show current, missing, expired and soon-to-expire credential records so managers can act before work is affected.

Can CertSync export mining workforce evidence?

Yes. Teams can export records by worker, contractor, company, document type, status or expiry window for client, site, audit and internal review workflows.

Does CertSync provide mining safety or legal advice?

No. CertSync is a credential, expiry and evidence-management platform. It does not provide mining safety advice, WHS consulting, legal advice or regulatory approval.

Is CertSync suitable for mining contractors?

Yes. CertSync is designed for contractor-heavy teams that need a clearer register for worker, subcontractor, site, document and expiry records.

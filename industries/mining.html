Mining compliance software FAQs

What mining workforce documents can CertSync track?

CertSync can track records such as site inductions, high-risk work licences, plant and operator tickets, permits, first aid certificates and contractor evidence where your team chooses to manage them.

Can managers see expired and soon-to-expire mining records?

Yes. CertSync is designed to show current, missing, expired and soon-to-expire credential records so managers can act before work is affected.

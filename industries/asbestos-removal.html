Asbestos evidence control

Asbestos removal paperwork is too important to leave scattered

Asbestos removal teams often manage licence evidence, training records, fit testing, SWMS, permits, clearances, insurance and contractor documents across multiple workers and projects. When those files sit in emails, folders and spreadsheets, it is hard to know what is current, missing or expired.

CertSync helps teams keep the evidence register organised without pretending to replace asbestos advice, WHS consulting, SWMS preparation or regulator guidance.

The practical question CertSync helps answer

Can we quickly see whether licence, training, SWMS, clearance, permit, insurance and contractor records are current, missing or expired, and produce stored evidence when requested?

Asbestos removal compliance FAQs

What is asbestos removal compliance software?

Asbestos removal compliance software helps teams organise asbestos-related licences, training evidence, SWMS, clearance records, permits, insurance, contractor documents and expiry dates in a central register.

What asbestos removal records can CertSync track?

CertSync can track Class A and Class B licence evidence, supervisor or assessor records, training certificates, fit testing evidence, SWMS, permits, clearance records, waste or disposal evidence, insurance documents and contractor records.

Can CertSync track asbestos clearances and supporting evidence?

Yes. CertSync can store clearance records and supporting evidence files, keep them connected to the relevant worker, contractor, company or site record, and help teams filter records by status or document type.

Can CertSync send asbestos licence expiry reminders?

Yes. CertSync can help teams manage renewal reminders for licences, training records, permits, certificates and other expiring evidence.

Can CertSync track subcontractor asbestos documents?

Yes. CertSync can help organise subcontractor company records, worker evidence, licences, insurance, SWMS, permits, clearances and onboarding documents.

Can we export asbestos records for builders or audits?

Yes. CertSync helps teams export stored evidence by worker, contractor, company, document type, status or expiry window for builder reviews, audits, insurer requests and internal checks.

Does CertSync provide asbestos removal or WHS advice?

No. CertSync is a compliance record, credential, expiry and evidence-management platform. It does not provide asbestos removal advice, WHS consulting, legal advice, SWMS writing or regulatory approval.

Is CertSync suitable for small asbestos removal teams?

Yes. CertSync is designed to help small and medium contractor-heavy teams manage worker, subcontractor, document and expiry records from one register.

Construction teams manage White Cards, high-risk work licences, first aid certificates, SWMS, insurances, inductions and site-specific documents. CertSync gives project and safety teams one place to see what is current, what is missing and what is expiring soon.

Common records CertSync can track

  • White Cards and site inductions

  • First aid, CPR and training certificates

Replace construction compliance spreadsheets

Construction compliance software FAQs

Can CertSync track White Cards and high-risk work licences?

Yes. CertSync can track White Cards, high-risk work licences, first aid certificates, permits, SWMS, inductions and other construction documents with expiry dates and evidence files.

Does CertSync guarantee WorkSafe compliance?

No software can guarantee regulatory compliance. CertSync supports credential record management, expiry tracking and reporting workflows so teams can keep evidence organised and easier to review.

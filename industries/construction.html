Construction document control

Construction compliance gets messy across workers, subcontractors and sites

Construction teams manage employees, subcontractors, White Cards, high-risk work licences, SWMS, permits, inductions, insurance documents and evidence requests from builders or principal contractors. When files sit across spreadsheets, email chains and shared folders, it is hard to know what is current, missing or expired.

CertSync gives project, operations and admin teams one register for the records they already manage, without pretending to replace WHS advice, SWMS preparation or regulator guidance.

The question CertSync helps answer

Construction compliance software FAQs

What is construction compliance software?

Construction compliance software helps teams organise worker, subcontractor, licence, induction, SWMS, permit, insurance and site evidence records so project teams can see what is current, missing or expired.

Can CertSync track White Cards and high-risk work licences?

Yes. CertSync can track White Cards, high-risk work licences, forklift tickets, first aid certificates, permits, SWMS, inductions and other construction documents with expiry dates and evidence files.

Can CertSync track construction SWMS and site documents?

Yes. CertSync can store SWMS, site-specific permits, inductions, method documents and supporting evidence while helping teams track ownership, status and review or expiry dates where needed.

Can CertSync track subcontractor documents?

Yes. CertSync can help organise subcontractor company records, worker credentials, insurances, licences, inductions, permits and evidence files in one register.

Can CertSync send construction licence expiry reminders?

Yes. CertSync can help teams monitor expiry dates and send reminders for licences, tickets, permits, insurance documents, training certificates and other time-sensitive records.

Can we export evidence for builders or audits?

Yes. CertSync helps teams export records by worker, subcontractor, company, credential type, status or expiry window for builder reviews, audits and internal checks.

Does CertSync provide WHS or legal advice?

No. CertSync is a credential, expiry and evidence-management platform. It does not provide WHS consulting, legal advice, SWMS writing or regulatory approval.

Is CertSync suitable for small construction contractors?

Yes. CertSync is designed to help small and medium contractor-heavy teams replace spreadsheet chasing with a central register for worker, subcontractor, document and expiry records.

Demolition compliance software FAQs

What demolition records can CertSync track?

CertSync can track demolition licences, SWMS, asbestos evidence, plant tickets, permits, inductions, training records, contractor documents and expiry dates.

Does CertSync provide WHS or demolition legal advice?

No. CertSync provides credential record management, expiry tracking and reporting tools. Teams should confirm demolition and WHS duties with the relevant regulator or adviser.

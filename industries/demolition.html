Demolition document control

Demolition compliance gets messy fast

Demolition work can involve employees, subcontractors, asbestos-related evidence, SWMS, plant operators, permits, inductions, insurance and last-minute evidence requests. When those records are spread across spreadsheets, shared drives, emails and text messages, it becomes hard to know what is current, what is missing and what has already expired.

CertSync gives your office, operations and site teams one register for the files and status checks they already manage, without pretending to replace WHS advice, SWMS preparation or regulator guidance.

The practical question CertSync helps answer

Can we quickly see whether worker, contractor, licence, SWMS, asbestos-related, plant ticket, induction, insurance and permit records are current, missing or expired, and produce stored evidence when someone asks?

Why not spreadsheets?

Spreadsheets work until one record is missed

A spreadsheet can list documents, but it usually separates the file from the status, the expiry date from the owner, and the contractor record from the worker record. That is where missed renewals and slow evidence requests creep in.

Spreadsheet chasing

  • Expiry visibility depends on manual upkeep
  • Hard to know who owns each record
  • Files live away from status fields
  • Evidence packs take time to build
  • Subcontractor records become messy
  • Expired or missing files are easy to overlook

CertSync register

  • Expiry dates and reminders stay attached to the document
  • Ownership is clearer across workers, contractors and companies
  • Files, status and notes live in one place
  • Evidence can be exported by useful filters
  • Contractor records stay connected to company profiles
  • Missing or expired records are easier to identify earlier

Demolition compliance software FAQs

What is demolition compliance software?

Demolition compliance software helps demolition businesses organise worker, contractor, licence, SWMS, permit, insurance, induction and site evidence records so teams can see what is current, missing or expired.

What demolition records can CertSync track?

CertSync can track SWMS, demolition method documents, asbestos registers, removal evidence, clearance records, licences, permits, plant tickets, inductions, insurance, training certificates, contractor company records and audit evidence.

Can CertSync track SWMS?

Yes. CertSync can store SWMS files, keep them connected to the relevant worker, contractor, company or site record, and help teams track status, ownership and expiry or review dates where needed.

Can CertSync track asbestos removal evidence?

Yes. CertSync can help store and organise asbestos-related evidence such as licences, registers, removal evidence, clearance records, permits, training records and contractor documents.

Can CertSync track subcontractor documents?

Yes. CertSync supports contractor and company records, including licences, insurance documents, SWMS, permits, onboarding evidence, inductions and other files your team needs to manage.

Can CertSync send expiry reminders?

Yes. CertSync can help teams track expiry dates and send reminders for licences, plant tickets, insurance documents, training certificates, permits and other time-sensitive records.

Can we export records for builders, auditors or insurers?

Yes. CertSync helps teams export stored evidence by worker, contractor, company, document type, status or expiry window for builder reviews, audits, insurer requests and internal checks.

Does CertSync provide WHS, legal or demolition advice?

No. CertSync is a compliance record, credential, expiry and evidence-management platform. It does not provide WHS consulting, legal advice, demolition advice, SWMS writing or regulatory approval.

Is CertSync suitable for small demolition businesses?

Yes. CertSync is designed to help small and medium contractor-heavy teams replace spreadsheet chasing with a central register for worker, subcontractor, document and expiry records.

Excavation compliance gets messy when plant, people and permits move fast

Civil excavation teams manage plant operators, subcontractors, excavation permits, high-risk work licences, SWMS, site inductions, insurance and evidence requests. When those files live across emails, shared folders and spreadsheets, it is hard to know what is current, missing or expired.

CertSync helps office, operations and site teams keep the evidence register organised without pretending to replace excavation advice, WHS consulting, SWMS preparation or regulator guidance.

Civil excavation compliance FAQs

What is civil excavation compliance software?

Civil excavation compliance software helps teams organise worker, plant, permit, SWMS, induction, insurance, subcontractor and site evidence records so managers can see what is current, missing or expired.

What civil excavation records can CertSync track?

CertSync can track excavation permits, plant and operator tickets, high-risk work licences, VOCs, SWMS, site inductions, service-location evidence, contractor documents, insurance and expiry dates.

Can CertSync track plant and operator tickets?

Yes. CertSync can store plant tickets, operator competency records, high-risk work licences, VOC evidence and related expiry dates in the worker or contractor record.

Can CertSync track excavation permits and SWMS?

Yes. CertSync can store excavation permits, SWMS, site documents and supporting evidence while helping teams track status, ownership and review or expiry dates where needed.

Can CertSync track subcontractor documents?

Yes. CertSync can help organise subcontractor company records, worker credentials, insurance documents, permits, SWMS, inductions and other evidence files.

Can CertSync help teams prepare for site or client checks?

Yes. CertSync helps teams keep worker and contractor evidence organised so records can be filtered and exported for site, client, builder, insurer or internal review.

Does CertSync provide excavation or WHS advice?

No. CertSync is a compliance record, credential, expiry and evidence-management platform. It does not provide excavation advice, WHS consulting, legal advice, SWMS writing or regulatory approval.

Is CertSync suitable for small civil contractors?

Yes. CertSync is designed to help small and medium contractor-heavy teams manage worker, subcontractor, document and expiry records from one register.

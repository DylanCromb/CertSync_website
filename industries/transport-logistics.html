Transport compliance software FAQs

What is transport compliance software?

Transport compliance software helps teams organise driver licences, medicals, permits, inductions, CoR training, contractor records, insurance documents and expiry dates in one register.

Can CertSync track driver and transport credentials?

CertSync can track driver licences, licence classes, permits, inductions, medicals, training certificates, CoR training evidence, contractor documents and renewal dates for transport and logistics teams.

Can CertSync track driver medicals and licence renewals?

Yes. CertSync can store driver medical evidence, licence files, licence classes and renewal dates where your team chooses to manage those records.

Can CertSync track contractor transport documents?

Yes. CertSync can help organise contractor company records, driver evidence, insurances, permits, inductions and other files your team needs to manage.

Can teams report on upcoming transport document expiries?

Yes. Teams can filter and report by worker, team, credential type, status and expiry window to identify records that need attention.

Can we export transport records for audits or clients?

Yes. CertSync helps teams export evidence by driver, contractor, company, document type, status or expiry window for audits, client requests, insurer reviews and internal checks.

Does CertSync provide transport legal or safety advice?

No. CertSync is a credential, expiry and evidence-management platform. It does not provide transport legal advice, Chain of Responsibility advice, WHS consulting or regulatory approval.

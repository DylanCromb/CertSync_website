Frequently Asked Questions

Q

Can I change my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

Q

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all plans. No credit card required to get started.

Q

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your data at any time.

Q

Do you offer custom pricing for large organizations?

Yes, we offer custom pricing and features for organizations with specific needs. Contact our sales team for more information.

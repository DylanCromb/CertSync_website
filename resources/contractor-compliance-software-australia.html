Contractor Compliance Software for Australian Businesses

Contractor compliance software helps businesses manage subcontractor licences, insurance, inductions, SWMS, permits, training records and expiry dates in one central system. It is useful when contractor evidence is spread across inboxes, spreadsheets, shared drives and project folders.

Why contractor records need a clear register

Contractor-heavy teams often need to know which company documents, worker licences, site inductions and insurance records are current before work starts. When each record lives in a different place, status checks become slow and evidence requests become harder to answer.

A central register makes it easier to see what is uploaded, current, missing, expired or expiring soon.

Who usually needs it

  • Operations and compliance managers
  • Project coordinators and site managers
  • Businesses using subcontractor crews
  • Teams responding to builder, insurer or audit evidence requests

Documents it can help track

Licences, insurance certificates, SWMS, inductions, permits, training records, company evidence and expiry dates.

Why spreadsheets struggle

Spreadsheets can list requirements, but they do not naturally keep evidence files, expiry dates, status and owners together.

Where CertSync fits

CertSync is suited to teams that need a central contractor and worker register with document tracking, expiry reminders and evidence exports.

What to look for in contractor compliance software

The software should help your team work with real records, not just tick boxes. The practical value comes from seeing files, expiry dates, status and ownership together.

Useful capabilities

  • Worker, contractor and company profiles
  • Document type and status tracking
  • Expiry reminders for licences, insurance and training
  • Evidence storage connected to each record
  • Exports for client, audit, insurer or internal review requests

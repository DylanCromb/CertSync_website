How Demolition Companies Manage SWMS and Licence Records

Demolition companies often need to manage SWMS, asbestos-related evidence, licences, plant tickets, permits, inductions, insurance and contractor records before work starts. The practical challenge is keeping those files current, reviewable and easy to produce when a builder, principal contractor, insurer, auditor or internal manager asks.

Why this matters in demolition

Demolition work can involve high-risk activities, changing site conditions, subcontractors, plant, asbestos-related evidence and multiple document owners. A static folder or spreadsheet can quickly fall behind the reality of the job.

A central register helps teams see which records are current, missing, expired or expiring before mobilisation and evidence requests.

Records often involved

  • SWMS and demolition method documents
  • Asbestos registers, removal evidence and clearances
  • Demolition-related licences, permits and registrations
  • Plant tickets, inductions, insurance and subcontractor files

SWMS records

Store SWMS files with the relevant worker, contractor, company or site record so the document is easier to find and review.

Licence and ticket records

Track expiry dates for licences, plant tickets, permits and training evidence before they become a mobilisation issue.

Asbestos-related evidence

Keep asbestos licences, registers, removal evidence, clearance records and supporting files organised in one register.

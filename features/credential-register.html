Credential register FAQs

What can be stored in the CertSync credential register?

Teams can store licences, permits, certifications, SWMS, training records, contractor documents, evidence files and expiry dates in the credential register.

Can CertSync replace a spreadsheet credential register?

CertSync is designed to replace manual spreadsheet registers with a shared system for credential details, evidence files, status, ownership and expiry tracking.

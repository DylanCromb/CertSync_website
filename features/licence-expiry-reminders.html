Licence expiry reminder FAQs

What reminder schedules can CertSync support?

CertSync can support default reminder windows such as 90, 60, 30 and 7 days before expiry, with schedules adjusted around your team's renewal workflow.

Can reminders go to both workers and managers?

Yes. CertSync is designed so worker and manager workflows can stay aligned around upcoming credential renewals and missing records.

Compliance reporting FAQs

What can CertSync compliance reports include?

CertSync reports can help teams review stored credential records by worker, contractor, team, document type, expiry window and current status.

Are CertSync reports a legal compliance guarantee?

No. CertSync helps organise and export credential records, but each organisation remains responsible for its compliance decisions, legal obligations and record review process.

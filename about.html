About CertSync

Simplifying credential management for businesses and employees worldwide

Our Mission

At CertSync, we believe that managing professional credentials shouldn't be complicated. Our mission is to provide a simple, secure, and efficient platform that helps both employees and employers keep credential records organised around industry requirements.

What We Do

CertSync is a credential, expiry and evidence-management platform for tracking professional licences, permits, certifications and workforce documents. Whether you are an individual professional or a growing organisation, CertSync helps keep records organised, searchable and easier to produce when they are requested.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, CertSync was born out of a simple observation: too many teams still manage worker credentials, contractor evidence and expiry dates across spreadsheets, inboxes and shared folders.

We built CertSync to give businesses a central register for the records they already rely on: licences, permits, SWMS, inductions, insurance documents, training evidence and renewal dates.
